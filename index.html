Overview

Lost items at DIU have been managed through shared Google Sheets — a quick fix, but far from user-friendly. From unsearchable entries to confusing formats, it’s easy to miss or misplace valuable reports.

Our goal? Replace that system with something clean, smart, and designed for students.

Research

We interviewed 15+ stakeholders

Before building, we asked real DIU students and faculty what they wanted from a lost and found system. Through informal interviews and shared forms, we gathered opinions, feature requests, and user pain points.

Features

For Users

  • Search for lost items by category, date, or location
  • Report found items with photos and details
  • Receive notifications when a match is found
  • View item history and contact information

For Admins

  • Manage all lost and found reports
  • Verify and approve item matches
  • Communicate with students via the platform
  • Generate reports on lost and found trends
